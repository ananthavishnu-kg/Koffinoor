About Koffinoor

Passion brewed into every detail since 2001

Our Story

Born from the aroma of tradition and the warmth of innovation, Koffinoor blends Indian authenticity with modern flair. Each cup is a handcrafted experience that celebrates our heritage and inspires new moments.

Since 2001, we have been more than just a café — we're a celebration of stories, cultures, and connections. Whether you're here for a bold shot of espresso or our signature Blood Coffee, we promise you an experience as rich and unforgettable as our name itself.

Our Heritage

Established 2001 • Refined Through Generations

At Koffinoor, we blend the soul of Indian coffee with a spark of modern brilliance. From timeless filter kaapi to bold innovations like our signature Blood Coffee, every brew tells a story.

Brewed to Shine. Crafted to Remember.

Koffinoor draws its essence from the legendary Kohinoor — a symbol of rarity, brilliance, and timeless value. Much like the famed diamond, our café is a hidden gem where every cup of coffee is crafted with care, passion, and a sparkle of uniqueness.

"Rooted in Indian warmth and enriched with global flavor, Koffinoor is more than just a café — it's a celebration of stories, cultures, and connections."

Our Values

Sustainability

We source our beans ethically and support sustainable farming practices that benefit both farmers and the environment. Every cup contributes to a better world.

Community

Koffinoor is more than a café — it's a gathering place where relationships are built over shared stories and great coffee. We're part of the neighborhood fabric.

Excellence

Like the Kohinoor diamond, we strive for perfection in every aspect — from bean selection to the final sip. Quality is never compromised.

Passion

Every cup is crafted with love and dedication by our skilled baristas who take pride in their craft. Passion is the secret ingredient in everything we do.


Our Mission

To create memorable coffee experiences that celebrate both traditional brewing methods and modern innovations, while fostering a community of coffee lovers who appreciate quality and craftsmanship.
